StackStats Apps LLC ("we", "our", "us") built SafetyBrief as a freemium application. This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

SafetyBrief stores all data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Specifically, the app may store: safety briefing records, incident reports, JSA documents, and toolbox talk notes. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server, third party, or analytics service.

No Account Required

SafetyBrief does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. You can use the app immediately upon download.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on external servers. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection tools.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we have no data to share with third parties. Period.

In-App Purchases

SafetyBrief may offer optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted storage. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed. We recommend using your device's built-in backup features (iCloud Backup) if you wish to preserve your data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require or collect any personal information from any user.
